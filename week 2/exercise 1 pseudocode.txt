4. Periksa kuku

  IF "Kuku siswa" value more than 5 AND less than 10

  ELSE IF "Kuku siswa" value more than 0 AND less than 5

  ADD "Jumlah siswa" value by 1

AFTER "Jumlah siswa" value equals to 40
DISPLAY "pengecekan kuku telah selesai"
